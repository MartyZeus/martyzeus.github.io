Background

Milestone Marketing Solutions (MMS) is a direct-to-consumer marketing firm, initially entirely focused on mail-based lead generation. When I was hired an 'Email Marketing Specialist', the company I had been undergoing a transition period after being acquired by Spectrum Automotive Holdings. My role was based around a sister company, Automotive Product Consultants (APC), offloading their email marketing programs to Milestone and a need for additional assistance in the transition and scaling of these programs. The programs involved partnering with automotive dealership groups and service contract administrators to market dealership customers with the goal of driving leads to a variety of call center. Over the first few months I mostly shadowed the creative director who act as the manager of the email marketing team. Both our team and APC's team worked out of Salesforce Marketing Cloud Engagement (SFMC) instance.

APC's processes involved exporting txt files of records from their in house CRM, transferring them via SFTP to the Marketing Cloud instance, importing the records into lists, and manually scheduling email send jobs for the week. Emails were built with chained if statements using dynamic content, ContentImageByKey() was used to dynamically load dealership logos into the emails, A/B testing was executed using SMFC's native testing feature. Our creative director had enhanced the program by implementing the use of Automation Studio to import, segment records by timezone using SQL queries, and send emails. He also enhanced the emails by dynamically personalize email content using AMPscript.

After gaining an understanding of the SFMC platform, taking Salesforce Trailhead courses, and getting my Marketing Cloud Engagement Foundations certification, I began taking a more active role in the automation and management of the Marketing Cloud instance. I started by working with our SQL Development team to set up an A/B testing process to maintain reporting at our SQL server.
